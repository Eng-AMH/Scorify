3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our CV analysis service
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send you verification codes (OTP) and account notifications
  • Send weekly digest emails (you can opt out in profile settings)
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and spam
  • Display your score history and achievements

We do not use your data to train AI models, sell advertising, or share with third parties for marketing.

4. Your CV Files

When you upload a CV, here's exactly what happens:

  • The file is temporarily stored on our server (PythonAnywhere)
  • Text is extracted from the file
  • The text is sent to Groq's AI API for analysis
  • The original file is immediately deleted from our servers after text extraction
  • Only the AI-generated analysis results are saved to your account

We do not permanently store your CV files. The extracted text sent to Groq's API is subject to Groq's privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq AI — processes CV text for analysis (groq.com)
  • Paddle — handles all payments and subscriptions (paddle.com)
  • PythonAnywhere — hosts our web application (pythonanywhere.com)
  • Google Fonts — loads web fonts
  • Google Translate — optional language translation feature

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in
  • CSRF token — protects against cross-site request forgery (security)
  • Theme preference — remembers dark/light mode
  • Language preference — remembers your chosen language

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies from third parties.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Delete — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correct — update or correct your personal information at any time
  • Portability — request your analysis history in a readable format
  • Opt out — unsubscribe from weekly digest emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: {{ support_email }}

8. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (Django's PBKDF2 algorithm)
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks
  • No hardcoded secrets — all credentials stored as environment variables
9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email for significant changes. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
