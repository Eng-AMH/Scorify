REFUND POLICY
Last updated: April 12, 2025 · Effective immediately

We want you to be happy with Scorify. If something goes wrong, we'll make it right. Read below to understand how our refunds work.

✅ 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribed to Pro or VIP and are not satisfied within the first 7 days of your subscription, we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Simply email us at {{ support_email }} within 7 days of your first payment.

When Refunds Apply
  • Within 7 days of your first subscription payment (new subscribers only)
  • If you were charged twice due to a technical error
  • If our service was completely unavailable for more than 24 continuous hours during your billing period
  • If a feature you were specifically promised was not delivered
When Refunds Do Not Apply
  • After 7 days from your first payment for that subscription period
  • For renewal charges (we send a reminder email 3 days before each renewal)
  • If you forgot to cancel before the renewal date
  • For partial months — subscriptions run for the full billing period
  • If your account was terminated for violating our Terms of Service
  • For the Free plan (it's free — no charges)
How to Request a Refund
1
Email us at {{ support_email }} with the subject line: "Refund Request"
2
Include your registered email address and the reason for the refund (optional but helpful)
3
We'll reply within 24 hours and process approved refunds within 5-10 business days through Paddle
Cancellations

Canceling your subscription is different from requesting a refund. When you cancel:

  • Your subscription stops renewing
  • You keep Pro/VIP access until the end of your current billing period
  • No refund is issued for the remaining days unless within the 7-day window

To cancel, go to Profile → Subscription → Cancel, or email us and we'll cancel it for you.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Paddle (our Merchant of Record). Refunds are issued back to your original payment method. Paddle may take 5-10 business days to process the refund depending on your bank.

For Paddle-related billing issues, you can also contact Paddle support directly at paddle.com.
